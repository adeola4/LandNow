What Our Network Works With

Every asset class our members are actively acquiring, managing, and placing.

Agricultural Land

Irrigated Tracts & Working Ranches

Our members source off-market agricultural parcels with appurtenant water rights — from Central Valley row-crop operations to Rocky Mountain grazing allotments. These aren’t listed on Land.com. They move through operator networks.

Water Infrastructure

Dam-Adjacent Rights & Storage Positions

Senior water rights tied to federal and state dam projects are a core focus. Unbundling storage rights from surface title creates two revenue streams from one position. Our due diligence frameworks verify priority dates and beneficial use history.

Rural Residential

Homesteads, Cabin Sites & Recreation Parcels

Lifestyle and self-reliance buyers find off-market parcels through the feed — mountain cabin sites with spring rights, river-access homesteads, and recreational tracts that cash-flow through lease arrangements while appreciating in value.

Water Rights

Senior Appropriations & Mitigation Credits

From pre-1914 appropriative rights in Colorado to wetland mitigation banking in the Southeast. Members access proprietary basin intelligence, legal framework reviews, and municipal lease structuring — turning paper water into cash-flowing positions.

Resource Extraction & Timber

Timber Harvest, Mineral Rights & Bundled Assets

Members acquire multi-generational timber tracts and resource bundles, restructuring them into tranches — harvest rights, conservation easements, and recreational access. The network provides the valuation frameworks and exit strategies.
